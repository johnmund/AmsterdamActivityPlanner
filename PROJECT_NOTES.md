## 3. The Six Routes (current progress)

All routes start from Keizersgracht 61. Status legend: ✅ confirmed location data · ⬜ needs map/itinerary build.

### Route 1 — Noord by free ferry: street art, Pekmarkt & brewery windmill ⬜
- Bike ~8 min to Centraal → **free NDSM ferry** (~every 15 min, bikes allowed, no ticket).
- **NDSM graffiti & street-art yard** — legal walls, ever-changing pieces around old shipyard.
- Lunch at **Pllek** — container-built beach café on the IJ waterfront, sand + deckchairs.
- Ferry back; if **Wed/Fri/Sat**, detour to **Pekmarkt** (Van der Pekstraat) — small local market.
- Market days: **Wed, Fri, Sat 10:00–16:00**.

### Route 2 — Eastern Islands & Brouwerij 't IJ ⬜
- Ride east along canals to the **Eastern Docklands** (modern architecture, footbridges).
- Loop past **Java-eiland** and **KNSM-eiland**.
- End at **Brouwerij 't IJ** — organic brewery at the foot of **De Gooyer** windmill.
- Tasting flight of 5 small glasses on the terrace.
- Hours: opens **14:00 weekdays / 12:00 weekends**.

### Route 3 — De Pijp + Albert Cuyp with Frankendael market finish ⬜
- Bike south to **De Pijp**; cruise **Albert Cuypmarkt** (Mon–Sat) — fresh stroopwafels etc.
- Drift through **Sarphatipark**.
- Continue east to **Park Frankendael** — old estate park, allotment gardens, manor house.
- **Best date: Sun 26 July** — Pure Markt sets up here, 11:00–18:00, free (food/music/crafts).

### Route 4 — Oud-West: De Hallen + Vondelpark concert afternoon ⬜
- Bike west to **De Hallen** — converted 1900s tram depot; food hall, indie shops, cinema.
- Lunch (bitterballen, croquettes, global street food).
- Into **Vondelpark** for the afternoon.
- **Openluchttheater** free summer performances: **Fri eve · Sat from 13:00 · Sun from 12:00**.
- Bring a picnic.

### Route 5 — Dappermarkt & Oosterpark (multicultural East) ⬜
- Bike east to **Dappermarkt** (Mon–Sat) in Oost — multicultural daily street market.
- Eats to try: Surinamese **bara**, **kibbeling** (fried fish).
- Cross into **Oosterpark** — city's oldest park.
- **Notable date: 1 July** — Keti Koti commemoration (abolition of slavery), from ~14:00.

### Route 6 — Kwaku Festival in Zuidoost (weekends only) ⬜
- Metro southeast to **Nelson Mandelapark**.
- **Kwaku** — multicultural summer festival (Surinamese-Antillean roots).
- Runs **weekends 11 July – 2 August**: street food, music, dance, football tournament.
- Food is the main event — go hungry.
