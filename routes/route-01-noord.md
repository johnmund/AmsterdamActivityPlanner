# Route 1 — Noord by free ferry

## Map sketch
- Start: Keizersgracht 61
- Route shape: home → Centraal → free NDSM ferry → NDSM street-art yard → Pllek → ferry back
- Optional add-on: Pekmarkt on Wed/Fri/Sat

## Suggested itinerary
- 09:00–09:30: Bike to Centraal and take the free ferry to NDSM
- 09:30–11:30: Explore the street-art yards and changing murals
- 11:30–13:00: Lunch at Pllek with a waterfront break
- 13:00–14:00: Ferry back toward the city center
- 14:00–16:00: Optional Pekmarkt detour if the day matches

## Best day
- Best on a Wednesday, Friday, or Saturday if you want the Pekmarkt add-on
- Good weekday fallback if you want a lighter, more flexible half-day

## Why this works well
- Low-friction transit with the ferry
- Good for a relaxed half-day with local energy
- Flexible enough to swap in weather backup or market time
