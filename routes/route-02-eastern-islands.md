# Route 2 — Eastern Islands and Brouwerij 't IJ

## Map sketch
- Start: Keizersgracht 61
- Route shape: home → canals east → Eastern Docklands → Java-eiland → KNSM-eiland → Brouwerij 't IJ

## Suggested itinerary
- 10:00–11:00: Bike east through the canal belt toward the Eastern Docklands
- 11:00–12:30: Loop through Java-eiland and KNSM-eiland for modern architecture and waterside views
- 12:30–14:00: Pause for lunch or a slow waterfront wander
- 14:00 onward: Finish at Brouwerij 't IJ for a tasting flight on the terrace

## Best day
- Best on a Friday or Saturday afternoon when the brewery terrace feels most relaxed
- Works well as a weekday afternoon if you want a slower pace

## Why this works well
- Feels contemporary and local rather than museum-heavy
- Easy to pair with a late-afternoon drink and a relaxed ride home
- Good fit for a weekday or weekend afternoon
