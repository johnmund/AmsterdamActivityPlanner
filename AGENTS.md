# AGENTS.md

## Project context
This workspace is for planning a July 2026 Amsterdam trip with self-guided city routes and itinerary ideas.

## Primary source of truth
- Use [PROJECT_NOTES.md](PROJECT_NOTES.md) as the main reference for goals, routes, dates, constraints, and open TODOs.
- Treat the notes as the authoritative planning context unless a user explicitly asks for a different source.

## Working conventions
- Keep the scope focused on Amsterdam city activities and walks; do not expand into major museum plans.
- Prefer route ideas that feel local and accessible rather than tourist-heavy.
- When adding or changing itinerary details, preserve the existing structure of the notes and clearly mark anything that still needs verification.
- Do not invent precise coordinates, event details, or opening hours unless the information is already documented or clearly presented as a TODO.

## Expected project structure
If new planning files are added, keep them organized around the existing intent:
- routes/ for route-specific content
- maps/ for map artifacts
- itinerary/ for day-by-day planning

## Helpful defaults
- Favor practical, low-friction logistics (bike-friendly routes, ferry/tram options, weather backup ideas).
- Keep suggestions grounded in the July 2026 dates and recurring market/event patterns already captured in the notes.
- When a choice is uncertain, leave a clear note rather than guessing.
